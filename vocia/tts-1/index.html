Learn how to integrate Text-To-Speech functionality into a Vocia system.

BACKGROUND

The Vocia TTS-1 converts text strings into audio signals, for the purpose of creating customized announcements. The TTS-1nc is a variant of the TTS-1 that is configured to integrate with Rauland Responder IV and 5 nurse call systems.
